Charcuteri

Description

This is a paragraph that is waxing poetic about how amazing all charcuteri boards are. Pick some of your favorite meats, cheeses and potentially crackers, cut them up into bite-sized pieces, place on a fancy board or a plate and enjoy

Ingredients

  • smoked meat
  • smoked cheese
  • soft cheese - brie
  • crackers
  • olives if you're into that kind of thing

Steps

  1. Cut things up
  2. Place strategically on a fancy board
  3. ENJOY!
