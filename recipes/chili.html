Chili Such Chili

Description

This is a paragraph that is waxing poetic about how amazing this damn chili recipe is. Place the ingredients in the bowl, simmer for hours and enjoy the aromas

Ingredients

  • ground meat
  • onions
  • diced tomatoes
  • garlic
  • chili powder

Steps

  1. Cook Meat
  2. Add and cook Onions and Garlic
  3. Add diced tomatoes and the chili powder
  4. Simmer until you are ready to eat
  5. Serve with your favorite chili additions
